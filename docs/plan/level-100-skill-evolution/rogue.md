# Night Fang Rogue — Dual-Wield Level 100 Plan

## Non-negotiable dual-wield identity

The Rogue uses two daggers by default. This is a gameplay, animation, equipment, and silhouette contract rather than a cosmetic flourish.

- The starter presentation mounts one dagger in each hand.
- Basic attacks alternate right and left weapon contacts, then use both blades for cross-cuts and finishers.
- Skill trails distinguish the hands: primary blade uses teal/steel; off-hand uses violet/shadow.
- Damage events must follow the contacting hand instead of duplicating one right-hand swing.
- Equipment power may still come from one weapon item slot initially, but the visual factory must create a paired off-hand model.
- Future loot may expose paired dagger bases without requiring a second equipment UI slot in the first implementation.

## Basic attack and Energy

Default dual wield, alternating contacts, and the current seven-hit vocabulary are shipped. Further milestone-specific basic/Energy transformations remain post-release work outside the Q/E/R/C evolution scope.

Seven-hit target vocabulary:

1. right-hand inward cut;
2. left-hand reverse cut;
3. alternating double stab;
4. low-high cross;
5. turning backhand pair;
6. forward step with four micro-cuts;
7. X-shaped dual-blade execution.

`Dagger Rush` must also be dual-wielded: alternating contact events, paired trails, and a two-blade finisher. Level growth raises path complexity and finisher quality rather than only hit count.

## Q — Twin Fang

| Gate | Evolution |
|---|---|
| Base | Right stab and left stab; both can apply bleed |
| Lv.20 | Add a simultaneous cross-cut as the third contact |
| Lv.40 | **Viper:** longer, stronger bleed / **Raptor:** faster contacts and higher critical focus |
| Lv.60 | A shadow echo repeats the cross-cut from behind the target area |
| Lv.80 | **Open Wound:** consumes bleed for a burst / **Heartseeker:** concentrates bonus damage on elites and bosses |
| Lv.100 | **Thousand Fang:** rapid paired stabs, invisible crossing cut lines, then synchronized wound detonation |

## E — Fan of Knives

| Gate | Evolution |
|---|---|
| Base | Both hands throw an overlapping forward fan |
| Lv.20 | Surviving knives return toward the two hands and can hit again |
| Lv.40 | **Black Fan:** broad field coverage / **Needle Line:** tight high-speed concentration |
| Lv.60 | Every second projectile creates a delayed shadow duplicate |
| Lv.80 | **Ricochet:** bounces between nearby targets / **Pinned Prey:** embeds knives in one durable target for detonation |
| Lv.100 | **Night Peacock:** forward twin fans expand into a ring and collapse back through the center |

## R — Shadow Frenzy

This is a damaging dash that opens a temporary attack-speed and movement-speed window.

| Gate | Evolution |
|---|---|
| Base | Dash through enemies with a two-blade path cut; gain four seconds of attack and move speed |
| Lv.20 | Buffed basic attacks produce a small alternating off-hand shadow hit |
| Lv.40 | **Ghost Rush:** movement and repositioning emphasis / **Red Tempo:** attack speed and critical emphasis |
| Lv.60 | Kills extend the window by 0.5 seconds, capped at two bonus seconds |
| Lv.80 | **Predator Flow:** shadow contacts chain between nearby enemies / **Boss Killer:** repeated hits ramp against one target |
| Lv.100 | **Beyond the Eye:** damaging entry dash, five-second dual-wield overdrive, and an exit explosion based on capped contact count |

Recommended caps:

- Attack speed: +25–40% by form and mutation.
- Movement speed: +20–35% by form and mutation.
- Animation time scale must remain synchronized with hit events.
- The buff cannot refresh itself indefinitely.

## Offensive keystones

Only the Level 100 bleed-tier Apex echo keystone is shipped. Levels 25, 50, and 75 remain post-release proposals.

| Level | Keystone |
|---|---|
| 25 | Completing an even-numbered dual-wield contact adds a small off-hand cut |
| 50 | Critical hits accelerate Energy gain within a capped window |
| 75 | Dashing through a bleeding enemy primes the next dual-blade finisher |
| 100 | Apex finishers duplicate one capped contact for each active bleed tier |
